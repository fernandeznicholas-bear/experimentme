# Experiment Me — Project Vision & Brief
> *The Science of You*

---

## What Is Experiment Me?

Experiment Me is a web platform and app that connects **psychological researchers** with **participants** — and fixes the fundamental broken exchange at the heart of research participation.

Today, research is a one-way transaction: participants give their time and data, and get nothing back. Experiment Me flips this into a **mutual value exchange**:

- **Participants** take validated, science-based psychological assessments and receive **immediate, personalized insight** — their scores, percentiles, and what the science actually says about people like them.
- **Researchers** gain access to a motivated, engaged, pre-profiled participant pool that produces higher quality data, faster recruitment, and richer baseline information.

**The core promise:** Every study on Experiment Me must give participants something real in return. Science you can see in yourself.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Traditional research participation platforms (Prolific, MTurk, SONA) treat participants like vending machine products — paid minimally, never informed of results, never invested in the outcome. This creates:

- Low engagement and poor data quality
- Participants who click through carelessly
- Zero loyalty or return participation
- A one-sided relationship that serves only the researcher

Experiment Me treats participants as **co-investigators in their own psychology.**

## How It Works

### For Participants
1. **Browse Studies** — Explore active research from verified universities and labs. Filter by topic, time commitment, and what insight you'll receive.
2. **Take the Assessment** — Answer validated, peer-reviewed psychological instruments. Not invented for clicks — published in journals.
3. **See Yourself Clearly** — Get results immediately: your score, your global percentile, what the science says about people who score like you, plain-English interpretation.
4. **Build Your Profile** — As you participate in more studies, your psychological profile grows — a living, evidence-based portrait of who you are over time.

### For Researchers
1. Post a study with IRB info, eligibility criteria, and what participants will receive back
2. Filter the participant pool by pre-collected trait scores, demographics, assessment history
3. Collect cleaner data from genuinely engaged participants
4. Export results, track completion rates, manage your study dashboard

## Assessments & Instruments

### Tier 1 — Free & Open Source (Launch Immediately)
These are public domain or openly licensed — no permission needed:

| Assessment | What It Measures | Questions | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SWLS** (Satisfaction with Life Scale) | Global life satisfaction | 5 | 2 min |
| **IPIP Big Five** | Personality (O,C,E,A,N) | 44 | 10 min |
| **Rosenberg Self-Esteem** | Self-worth | 10 | 2 min |
| **Grit Scale** (Duckworth) | Perseverance & passion | 12 | 3 min |
| **Growth Mindset Scale** (Dweck) | Beliefs about ability | 16 | 4 min |
| **PERMA Profiler** (Seligman) | Five pillars of flourishing | 23 | 5 min |
| **Subjective Happiness Scale** | Happiness self-assessment | 4 | 1 min |
| **DASS-21** | Depression, anxiety, stress | 21 | 5 min |

### Tier 2 — Partnership Targets
Sites like **VIA Character Strengths**, **Authentic Happiness** (UPenn), and **16Personalities** have their own portals but no research-participant bridge.

**Our pitch to them:**
> "We send you users who complete your assessment. In return, you gain access to our research infrastructure and a motivated participant pipeline. Researchers can recruit participants who've already completed your scale — pre-screened, engaged, motivated. You get warm, qualified, psychologically-profiled users."

### Tier 3 — Future Licensing
Proprietary tools (NEO-PI-3, MBTI, DISC) charge licensing fees but have API programs. Once we have users, we negotiate data-sharing agreements or revenue splits.

## Multilingual Strategy

Build multilingual support from day one — don't retrofit it later.

- Most IPIP instruments are already validated and translated into 25+ languages
- Published translated versions of major scales exist in peer-reviewed journals (no translation needed — use validated versions)
- **Spanish-first** is a massive strategic advantage — no major research platform prioritizes Spanish speakers
- El Paso / US-Mexico border region is the natural first market

**Language rollout:** English → Spanish → Portuguese → French → German → Mandarin

## Design Direction

- **Aesthetic:** Warm & human — earthy tones, approachable, not clinical
- **Colors:** Terracotta (`#C4704F`), Sage (`#7A9B76`), Amber (`#D4A853`), Deep Brown (`#2C1810`), Cream (`#F7F0E8`)

## Tech Considerations

- **Frontend:** HTML/CSS/JS to start; migrate to React as complexity grows
- **Backend (Phase 2):** Supabase recommended — handles auth, database, and API in one place, beginner-friendly
- **Hosting:** Vercel (same setup as waddle.money)
- **Domain:** experimentme.com (owned via GoDaddy)
- **CRM / Waitlist:** Start with Mailchimp or Buttondown (free to start); upgrade later

---

## Key Ethical Considerations

- Every instrument used must be properly attributed to original authors
- IRB compliance framework needed before launching live research studies
- Transparent data privacy policy — participants own their data
- Results must include appropriate caveats (not clinical diagnoses)
- DASS-21 and similar clinical-adjacent scales need careful framing
